Remote Support

Cam's tool for remote support.

Download the Rust Client

Chrome Users: Click the download button and save the executable to your Downloads folder

Edge Users: Click the download button and the executable will automatically be saved into your Downloads folder 


Run the Rust Client 

Chrome Users: Click on the download arrow at the top right of your chrome window and select the Rust Client executable

Edge Users: Press CTRL+J and then select the Rust Client executable from the downloads dropdown that opens on your screen

Relay Infomation to Cam

Once the client has opened, you will need to read your ID and Password to Cam to provide him with remote access.
